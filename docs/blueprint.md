# **App Name**: Shear Bliss

## Core Features:

- Appointment Scheduling: Users can select a date and available time slot to book appointments.
- Staff Selection: Users can view staff profiles and choose their preferred staff member when booking.
- Service Catalog: Display a structured catalog of services with descriptions, duration, and pricing.
- Queue Management: Provide a live queue status showing the userâ€™s position and estimated waiting time.
- Automated Confirmation: Send an automatic confirmation email to the user after a successful booking.
- Staff Rating and Reviews: Enable users to rate and review staff members after their appointments.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA) to evoke a sense of relaxation and luxury.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F5F5F5), almost white, for a clean and modern feel.
- Accent color: Pale rose (#FFB6C1) to highlight key actions and call-to-actions.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font that provides an elegant and contemporary feel.
- Use refined and minimal line icons to represent services and actions.
- Employ a clean, grid-based layout with ample white space for easy navigation and a focus on content.
- Subtle transition effects for a smooth and engaging user experience when navigating between sections and booking steps.
